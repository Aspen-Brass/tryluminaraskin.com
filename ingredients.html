Skincare Education

Ingredient Guide

Learn about the key cosmetic ingredients in Luminara Skin products. Each ingredient is selected for its ability to support healthy-looking, radiant skin.

Featured Peptides

Syn-Ake

Cosmetic Tripeptide

Syn-Ake is a cosmetic tripeptide inspired by temple viper research. In topical skincare formulas, it is used to help soften the appearance of expression lines and support smoother-looking skin.

Found in: Rewind Peptide Serum

Learn more about Syn-Ake →

GHK-Cu

Copper Peptide Complex

GHK-Cu is a cosmetic copper tripeptide known for supporting smoother-looking, more refined skin. It helps improve the appearance of skin texture and tone.

Found in: Rewind Peptide Serum

Learn more about GHK-Cu →

Peptide Complexes

Multi-Peptide Blends

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that serve as building blocks for proteins in the skin. In cosmetic formulations, they help support the appearance of firmer, more youthful-looking skin.

Found in: Rewind, Revive, Restore

Learn more about Peptides →

Hydration & Moisture

Hyaluronic Acid

Humectant

A widely used cosmetic humectant that helps the skin look hydrated and plump. Hyaluronic acid can hold up to 1000x its weight in water, helping to maintain skin moisture levels.

Found in: Rewind, Revive, Renew

Aloe Vera

Botanical Extract

A soothing botanical extract known for its hydrating and calming properties. Helps maintain skin comfort and supports a healthy-looking complexion.

Found in: Remove Gentle Cleanser

Vitamins & Antioxidants

Retinol

Vitamin A Derivative

A derivative of Vitamin A commonly used in cosmetic formulations to support skin renewal and improve the appearance of fine lines and texture. One of the most studied cosmetic ingredients.

Found in: Restore Night Cream

Vitamin C

Antioxidant

A powerful antioxidant that helps brighten the appearance of skin and supports a more even-looking complexion. Helps protect the skin from environmental stressors.

Found in: Renew Day Moisturizer

Niacinamide

Vitamin B3

A cosmetic ingredient known for helping improve the appearance of pores, uneven skin tone, and supporting the skin's natural barrier function.

Found in: Restore Night Cream

Caffeine

Botanical Extract

A botanical extract commonly used in eye care products to help reduce the appearance of puffiness and support a more awake, refreshed look around the eye area.

Found in: Revive Eye Serum

Soothing & Calming

Chamomile

Botanical Extract

A gentle botanical extract known for its soothing properties. Helps calm and comfort the skin, making it ideal for sensitive skin types.

Found in: Remove Gentle Cleanser

Micellar Water

Cleansing Technology

A gentle cleansing technology that uses tiny oil molecules (micelles) suspended in soft water to attract dirt, oil, and makeup without harsh rubbing or rinsing.

Found in: Remove Gentle Cleanser
