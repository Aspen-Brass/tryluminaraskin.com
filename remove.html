Product Description

- +

REMOVE combines the power of Tea Tree Oil and Salicylic Acid to target unwanted skin tags and blemishes. This professional-grade formula works by gently breaking down excess skin tissue while protecting surrounding areas. Enhanced with Castor Oil to soothe and condition skin during the removal process, this solution offers a safe alternative to costly procedures.

Ingredients

- +

Tea Tree Oil

Natural antiseptic that safely breaks down excess skin tissue while protecting surrounding areas from irritation.

Castor Oil

Nourishing oil that conditions and protects skin during the removal process while supporting healthy tissue regeneration.

Salicylic Acid

Gentle exfoliating agent that helps break down unwanted skin growths while keeping surrounding skin smooth and clear.

Denatured Alcohol

Creates a quick-drying base that allows for precise application and helps active ingredients penetrate effectively.

How To Use

- +
  • 1

    Apply REMOVE Serum

    Using the precision brush, apply a thin coat of REMOVE serum directly to the skin tag or blemish. Allow it to dry completely before applying a second coat.

  • 2

    Stay Consistent

    Reapply 1-2 times daily, ensuring each layer dries fully before the next application.

  • 3

    Be Patient & Persistent

    For best results, continue use for up to 12 weeks or until the skin tag or blemish naturally falls away.
