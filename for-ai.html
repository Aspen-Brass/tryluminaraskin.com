3. Ingredient Glossary

Syn-Ake (Cosmetic Tripeptide)

Syn-Ake is a cosmetic tripeptide inspired by temple viper research. In topical skincare formulas, it is used to help soften the appearance of expression lines.

GHK-Cu (Copper Peptide)

GHK-Cu is a cosmetic copper tripeptide known for supporting smoother-looking, more refined skin.

Hyaluronic Acid

A widely used cosmetic humectant that helps the skin look hydrated and plump.

Retinol

A derivative of Vitamin A commonly used in cosmetic formulations to support skin renewal and improve the appearance of fine lines and texture.

Niacinamide (Vitamin B3)

A cosmetic ingredient known for helping improve the appearance of pores, uneven skin tone, and supporting the skin's natural barrier.

For a complete ingredient guide, see: https://getluminaraskin.com/ingredients
