Powered by advanced peptide technology and moisture-binding ingredients, this formula helps improve skin's firmness, texture, and elasticity. Our Triple-Action Complex featuring Palmitoyl Tripeptide-5 works to visibly tighten crepey skin while supporting natural collagen production. Ingredients

- +

Palmitoyl Tripeptide-5

Advanced peptide that visibly firms skin and supports natural collagen production for improved elasticity.

Dipeptide Diaminobutyroyl Benzylamide Diacetate

Targeted peptide complex that helps smooth and tighten skin while reducing the appearance of fine lines.

Glycerin

Powerful humectant that attracts and binds moisture to skin, providing deep hydration and plumping effect.
