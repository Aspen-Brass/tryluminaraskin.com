Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which products are right for my skin type?

Luminara products are designed to work for all skin types — including dry, oily, combination, and sensitive skin. Each formula is made with clinically studied and proven ingredients that support healthy, balanced skin without irritation. Whether your goal is hydration, brightening, or anti-aging, you can feel confident that every Luminara product is formulated to deliver visible results safely and effectively for your unique complexion.

How do I apply my products?
  • Rewind: apply Rewind to clean, dry skin morning and evening. Use 2-3 drops and gently dab and smooth over into skin. Allow 30 seconds for absorption before applying other products. (can be paired with Revive)
  • Revive: Apply 2-3 drops to clean skin, wait 30 seconds. (can be paired with Rewind)
  • Restore: Mix 1 scoop with 8 oz water. Take daily.
  • Renew: Apply generous amounts to the Chest, Neck, and Arms dailiy.
Are your products safe for sensitive skin?

Yes. Luminara products are made with clinically tested, science-backed ingredients proven to be gentle and effective for all skin types, including sensitive skin. Each formula is carefully balanced to deliver visible results without harsh irritants or unnecessary additives.

If you have particularly reactive or delicate skin, we recommend performing a patch test before full application. Simply apply a small amount of the product to a discreet area (such as the inside of your wrist or behind your ear) and wait 24 hours to check for any irritation. This ensures your skin feels completely comfortable with the formula.

How do I perform a patch test?

To perform a patch test, apply a small amount of the product to a discreet area of skin — such as the inside of your wrist or just behind your ear. Leave it on for 24 hours and observe for any signs of redness, itching, or irritation. If no reaction occurs, it’s safe to begin using the product as directed. This simple step helps ensure your skin is comfortable with new formulations, especially if you have sensitive or reactive skin.

Can I use multiple Luminara products together?

Yes. Luminara products are formulated to work synergistically, enhancing results when used together as part of a daily routine. Each product serves a unique purpose — from renewal and hydration to protection and repair — and can be layered safely and effectively for optimal skin health and visible results.

For best results, follow our recommended usage guides or refer to the “How do I incorporate your products into my routine?” section below.

How do I incorporate your products into my routine?
  • Rewind:
  • AM: Cleanser → Rewind → Moisturizer → SPF
  • PM: Cleanser → Rewind → Retinol or Retinoid → Night Cream
  • Revive:
  • AM only: Cleanser → Rewind → Revive → Moisturizer → SPF
  • Restore:
  • AM only: Restore (mix with water) → Cleanser → Rewind → Revive → Moisturizer → SPF
  • Renew:
  • PM only: Cleanser → Rewind → Renew → Moisturizer or Night Cream for Neck, Chest, and Arms.
When will I see results?

Most users begin to see and feel improvements within the first few weeks of consistent use. Your skin’s exact response may vary based on your unique skin type and routine. Luminara products are made with clinically tested, science-backed ingredients that deliver both immediate benefits (like hydration and smoother texture) and long-term results with continued use — including improved tone, firmness, and overall radiance.

For best results, use your Luminara products consistently, morning and night, as part of your daily skincare routine.

Can I use Rewind with my retinol?

Yes — Rewind can be safely used with retinol or retinoid products as part of your evening routine. Rewind helps prep and balance the skin, supporting hydration and barrier strength, which can actually help reduce dryness or irritation sometimes caused by retinol use.

For best results, apply Rewind first after cleansing, allow it to fully absorb, then follow with your retinol or retinoid, and finish with a night cream or moisturizer. If you’re new to retinol, start by alternating nights and increase frequency as your skin builds tolerance.
